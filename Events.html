Our Events

MESA hosts a multitude of events catered to empower not only the Industrial knowledge of the students, but their 
personal skill development as well. Properly planned with the industrial representatives, our events intend to prepare 
the students to be more workforce ready as we keep them up with the pace of industrial growth

The workshop was held with the motive to enhance the students' personal skillset as we believe Adobe Photoshop is an essential software in social branding which is the new norm of marketing strategy in this era. We invited our former MESA President, Mr. Cheah Heng Yao, who is experienced in the designing software to share on the tips on some fundamental skills.

TRACTION POWER SUPPLY

22 December 2021  |  2 Hours |  Industrial Talk

Initiated by Vitrox Sdn Bhd, the talk focused on introduction to inside and outside of digital camera, digital image acquisition tools, digital camera-computer interface, types of sensors used and result of image acquisition, which basically explains every step from sensor to digital image. The talk was hosted by two managers from the R&D department of Vitrox, which are Mr. Chong Wei Chin and Mr. Lee Sung Way and students had the opportunity to learn the usage of machine vision in fault detection and the processes involved.
